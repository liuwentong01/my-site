## Deep Work

Cal Newport makes a compelling case for cultivating deep work habits in an increasingly distracted world.

### Key Takeaways

- Deep work is becoming increasingly rare and increasingly valuable
- The ability to perform deep work is a skill that must be trained
- Embrace boredom - don't reach for your phone every free moment

### My Rating: 5/5

This book fundamentally changed how I think about productivity and focus.
