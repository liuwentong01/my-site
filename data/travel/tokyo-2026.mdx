## Tokyo Trip

Tokyo is a city of contrasts - ancient temples sit next to neon-lit skyscrapers.

### Day 1: Asakusa and Senso-ji

Started the trip with a visit to Senso-ji, Tokyo's oldest temple. The Nakamise-dori shopping street was full of traditional snacks and souvenirs.

### Day 2: Shibuya and Harajuku

The famous Shibuya crossing is as impressive in person as it looks in photos.
