## New Words

- **ephemeral** /ɪˈfemərəl/ - lasting for a very short time
- **ubiquitous** /juːˈbɪkwɪtəs/ - present, appearing, or found everywhere
- **paradigm** /ˈpærədaɪm/ - a typical example or pattern of something

## Sentences

1. The ephemeral beauty of cherry blossoms reminds us to cherish the moment.
2. Smartphones have become ubiquitous in modern society.
